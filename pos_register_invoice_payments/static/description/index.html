Register invoice payments in Odoo!!

Maintaining invoice for each and every order in POS is quite important for every business out there. It enables you to keep a track of all the records of orders placed at POS till the current date. Also, registering invoice payments in a running POS Session is necessary so that the POS users can immediately save the invoice payment in the running session itself without forgetting to do it later. With the help of "Odoo POS Register Invoice Payments" module, POS User has an option to view, unreconcile or register invoice payment in running Point Of Sale Session. This module is good for scenarios if the seller wishes to register invoice payment at POS end.

Detailed Features List


Manage the list of invoices in running POS Session

  • A seller can view the list of invoices associated with his customers directly from a running POS session.
  • A seller can register invoice payments as well as view invoice payments in running POS Session.


Register the payments of customers in Odoo

  • Sellers can unreconcile invoice payments at POS end.
  • A seller can register invoice payment either manually or by Outstanding credits of associated customers.
