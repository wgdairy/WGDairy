POS Invoice Details

Easily view the invoice details of customers in POS!!

Odoo POS Invoice Details Module allows the seller to view the list of all the invoices that are associated with a customer directly from a running Point Of Sale session. Admin can select a particular invoice from the list to view the complete details. Moreover, this module works in POS offline mode as well.

View the invoice details with just a few clicks in POS!!

Imagine, you are closing the POS Session but facing a confusion regarding total sales of that particular day. You are not able to remember the invoice details of customers with whom you had a confusion. In that case, you will be collecting receipts and searching for that one particular order.

To avoid this situation, Odoo POS Invoice Details Module lists down all the POS Orders for you. Now, you can easily look for the invoice details of your customers at the POS frontend itself. Also, there is no need to be online on POS. You can view the invoice details of the customers in the offline mode as well.

Detailed Features List

View the list of invoices at POS in Odoo

  • Seller can view the list of invoices associated with his customers directly from a running POS session.
  • The POS user can select a particular invoice from the list to view the invoice details.

Search invoice as per its details

  • Sellers can search invoices on the basis of the invoice number, invoice date or sales person.

View the complete details of the invoice in Odoo

  • Select an invoice from the list to view the complete details.
  • No complex configuration is required. Just install and use.

Works at offline mode too

  • The Module works even when the POS is offline.
  • The POS user can easily view the invoice details even if the system is not connected from the Internet network.
